# Artistic statement

At first, it felt as though the place had erected a silent barrier, refusing to let me in. My first three visits yielded nothing—I plunged deeper into its immense visual potential, yet left without a single worthy frame. I challenged its heavy geometries across multiple mediums, shooting on 135 film, 6x9 medium format, and 4x5" large format. It was only on the fourth journey that the ancient sandstone sanctuary near Melitopol finally opened up, granting me a handful of striking, honest images.


Once, I was fortunate enough to wander through its monolithic maze during a dense, heavy mist. In this landscape, human presence is fleeting; rare visitors appear on the periphery, only to dissolve instantly back into the gray stone, leaving behind nothing but disembodied voices that quietly vanish into the air. In those muted moments, the boundary between eras blurs, and you can almost hear the rhythmic breath of eternity.

As for the historical reality of this place, I know very little—I have heard fragments of its archaeological past, but chose not to remember a single word. I prefer the clean state of observation. Across thousands of years, a forgotten prehistoric steppe civilization left its marks here: I see ancient stone effigies (babas) guarding the horizon, and hand-cut millstones resting in the grass—proof that they once harvested grain and lived by the soil. Stonetomb is a complex, demanding site filled with unanswered questions. It is a lingering ache that, were it not for the war, I would have returned here at least a dozen times before publishing this series in 2026. Given the chance, I will undoubtedly go back.

# Глава 1: «Каменный Барьер» (The Monolithic Threshold)

Состав: DP2Q2173.tif, DP2Q2181.tif, DP2Q2192.tif, DP2Q2200.tif.Что это: Масштабные пейзажи песчаниковых глыб под огромным небом степи. Те самые первые кадры, передающие физическое ощущение преграды. Место кажется хаотичным, чужим, неприступным грудным хребтом земли.

# Глава 2: «Призраки в Тумане» (Voices in the Mist)

Состав: DP2Q2918.tif, DP0Q0398.tif, DP0Q0401.tif, DP0Q0408.tif, DP0Q0409.tif.Что это: Кадры, где контраст падает, жесткие тени исчезают, а камни начинают буквально «растворяться» в пространстве, как и голоса редких посетителей. Здесь рождается то самое «дыхание вечности», о котором вы пишете.

# Глава 3: «Забытая Цивилизация» (Traces of the Forgotten)

Состав: DP2Q2948.tif, DP2Q2949.tif, DP2Q2953.tif, DP2Q2956.tif, DP2Q2959.tif, DP2Q2962.tif.What it is: Самая фактурная, овеществленная часть. Появление каменных жерновов, круглых рукотворных форм и силуэтов антропоморфных степных изваяний. Это свидетельство древнего труда — выращивания хлеба, ритуалов, замершего времени.

# Глава 4: «Эпилог: Недосказанность» (The Unanswered Questions)
